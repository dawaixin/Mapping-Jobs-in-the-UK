Introduction

Research aiming to gain insight into the labour market has for the most part relied upon the design and exploitation of various arbitrary categories to reflect the different kinds of employment available within an area. This project aims to show how Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques can be used as additional tools in the study of the employment sector.

The website presents the results of experimentation using two different models on a large dataset (3.3 million) of job adverts. The models allowed the classification of adverts into different categories corresponding to employment sectors. The jobs were localised into different areas of the UK using the information provided in the adverts.
